I‘m a third-year undergraduate student from [College of Control Science and Engineering](http://www.cse.zju.edu.cn/cseenglish/){:target="_blank"}
, [Zhejiang University](https://www.zju.edu.cn/english/){:target="_blank"}. My research interests include computer vision and machine learning. At present, I'm dedicated to exploring MOT(Multiple Object Tracking) and strive to uncover the intrinsic connections between specific objects across different frames. Through my research, I hope to contribute to the development of more advanced and effective tracking methods that can recognize and follow multiple objects in a continuous and seamless manner. Additionally, I have a keen interest in understanding how deep neural networks work and how we can interpret their decisions. If an opportunity arises in the future, I hope to specialize in this area of study and contribute to its advancement.
